What's "High Usage Times"? Yield is free to use. But hosting (Cloudflare) only stays free up to a monthly budget. When traffic gets so high that it would start costing real money, Yield switches to High Usage Times: generation pauses for free accounts until the next month, while Priority members keep full access — their $20/mo is exactly what funds those busy periods.
