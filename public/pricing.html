Questions

What are "High Usage Times"?

Yield's hosting is free up to a monthly budget. When traffic gets high enough that it would start costing real money to host, Yield enters High Usage Time: free generation pauses until the next month so the app stays free to run. Priority members keep full access the whole time — their subscription is exactly what funds those busy periods.

Will I ever be charged unexpectedly?

No. Free is free. The only charge is if you choose Priority Access ($20/mo), which you can cancel anytime from your account.

Do I keep my projects if I'm on the free plan?

Yes. Your projects are saved to your account and (if you connect GitHub) your code lives in your own repos — you own it regardless of plan.

Which AI models does Yield use?

Six coder models — Kimi K2.6, MiniMax M3, DeepSeek V4 Flash, Step 3.7 Flash, DeepSeek V4 Pro, and GLM 5.1 — plus an Auto router (gpt-oss-20b) that picks the best one per prompt. Every prompt is screened by an automatic safety guard first.
