Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026. Plain-language template — review with a professional before launch.

What we collect

Account basics from your sign-in provider (GitHub or Google): your name, email, avatar, and provider id. Projects you create and their generated code. Anonymous usage counters to enforce fair-use limits. Billing identifiers from Stripe if you subscribe.

How prompts are processed

Prompts are sent to third-party AI models to generate apps, and to an automated safety classifier for screening. We don't sell your data. Don't put secrets or sensitive personal data into prompts.

GitHub access

If you connect GitHub, we request permission to create and write to repositories so we can store your generated code. Your access token is encrypted at rest and used only to sync your code. You can revoke access anytime in your GitHub settings.

Cookies

We use a signed session cookie to keep you logged in and an anonymous device cookie to apply free-tier limits. No third-party advertising cookies.

Payments

Payments are handled by Stripe; we never see your full card details. We store only the Stripe customer/subscription identifiers needed to manage your plan.

Your choices

You can delete projects anytime, disconnect GitHub, cancel Priority Access, and request account deletion by contacting us.
